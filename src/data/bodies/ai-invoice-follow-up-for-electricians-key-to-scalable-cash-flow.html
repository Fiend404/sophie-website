The Cash Flow Challenge for Electricians

Australian electrical businesses face unique cash flow pressures. Materials need to be purchased upfront, apprentices need to be paid weekly, and clients often delay payment for 30, 60, or even 90 days.

The result? A profitable business on paper that struggles to keep enough cash in the bank to operate day-to-day. AI invoice follow-up breaks this cycle by ensuring payments are chased consistently and professionally.
