Why Plumbing Bills Go Unpaid

Most clients don't intend to dodge payment. Life gets busy, the invoice gets buried in their inbox, and suddenly it's been three weeks. For plumbers running a small crew, every unpaid invoice creates a cash flow gap that ripples through the entire operation.

The traditional approach of calling clients or sending manual emails is time-consuming and inconsistent. Some invoices get followed up, others slip through the cracks.

How AI Recovers What You're Owed
